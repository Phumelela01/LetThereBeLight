Let There Be Light

Community-powered work. Dignity. Inclusion. Light.

Creating opportunities for dignity, income, and inclusion for people with disabilities through community-powered work in Ezibeleni, Queenstown.

Our Story

Born from Community

Let There Be Light emerged from the heart of Phumelela Special Care Centre, but with a vision entirely its own. We are a cooperative that believes in the power of community-driven work to transform lives.

Every person with a disability deserves dignity, purpose, and the opportunity to contribute meaningfully to their community. Through our cooperative model, we create pathways to independence and inclusion.

Our Values

Dignity in every opportunity
Community-powered solutions
Inclusion for all abilities
Sustainable growth

What We Do

Our cooperative offers diverse opportunities for skill development, income generation, and community contribution.

Handmade Goods

Beautiful crafts and intricate beadwork created with skill and passion. Each piece tells a story of creativity and determination.

Skills Development

Comprehensive training programs and micro-enterprise development to build confidence and capabilities.

Agro-processing

Sustainable farming including pig and poultry operations that provide fresh, local produce to our community.

Catering Services

Delicious, locally-sourced meals prepared with care for events and community gatherings.
